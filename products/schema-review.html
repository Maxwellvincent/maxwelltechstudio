The problem
Anki reviews concepts. Schema Review reviews understanding.

Flashcards test whether you recognize an answer. This tests whether you can reconstruct the whole mental model from nothing.

Flashcard-only approach
  • Cued recall — the question is already half the answer
  • Reviews isolated facts, not connected schemas
  • Easy to pass cards while not understanding the concept
  • No signal on whether you could actually explain it
Schema Review approach
  • Free recall — reconstruct everything from a blank slate
  • Reviews the whole concept: mechanisms, connections, applications
  • Feynman grading — if you can't teach it, you don't know it
  • FSRS v5 schedules the next review based on actual retention
